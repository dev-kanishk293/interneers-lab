RETURN POLICY

Thank you for shopping with us. We strive to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase. This return policy outlines the terms and conditions under which products may be returned, refunded, or replaced.

1. GENERAL RETURN WINDOW
Customers may initiate a return within 7 days of receiving the product. Any return requests made after this period may not be accepted unless explicitly approved under special circumstances.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR RETURNS
To be eligible for a return:
- The product must be unused and in its original packaging.
- All accessories, manuals, and included items must be returned.
- The product should not show signs of wear, damage caused by misuse, or tampering.

3. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If a product arrives damaged or defective:
- Customers are eligible for a full refund or replacement.
- A return request must be initiated within 48 hours of delivery.
- Customers must provide clear photographic or video evidence showing the damage.
- Our support team will review the claim before approving a replacement or refund.

4. REFUNDS
Refunds are processed after the returned item is inspected:
- Approved refunds will be credited to the original payment method.
- Refund processing may take 5–7 business days after approval.
- Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part.

5. REPLACEMENTS
Customers may request a replacement instead of a refund:
- Replacement is subject to product availability.
- If unavailable, a refund will be issued instead.

6. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following items cannot be returned:
- Perishable goods
- Customized or personalized products
- Items marked as "Final Sale"

7. RETURN PROCESS
To initiate a return:
- Contact customer support with your order ID.
- Provide reason for return and supporting evidence if applicable.
- Follow instructions provided by our support team.

8. CANCELLATION POLICY
Orders can be canceled before shipment. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible and the return policy will apply.

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time without prior notice.
