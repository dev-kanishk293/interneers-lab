PRODUCT MANUAL: LEGO CASTLE SET

Thank you for purchasing the Lego Castle Set. This manual provides instructions for safe usage, assembly, and maintenance.

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
The Lego Castle Set includes:
- 500 high-quality interlocking pieces
- Instruction booklet
- Decorative elements such as flags, shields, and mini-figures

2. AGE RECOMMENDATION
This product is recommended for children aged 10 years and above due to small parts that may pose a choking hazard.

3. ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS
- Follow the step-by-step guide included in the box.
- Sort pieces by size and type before starting.
- Assemble on a flat surface to prevent losing pieces.
- Adult supervision is recommended for younger users.

4. SAFETY GUIDELINES
- Do not ingest or place small parts in the mouth.
- Keep away from pets.
- Avoid applying excessive force while assembling parts.

5. WARRANTY INFORMATION
The Lego Castle Set comes with a 6-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects only.
The warranty does not cover:
- Damage caused by misuse
- Missing pieces due to user handling
- Wear and tear from regular usage

6. MAINTENANCE
- Store pieces in a dry and cool place.
- Clean using a dry cloth. Avoid water exposure.
- Keep all components organized to prevent loss.

7. CUSTOMER SUPPORT
For support, contact our service team with your order ID and product details.

8. ADDITIONAL FEATURES
- Compatible with other Lego sets
- Expandable design for creative building
- Includes themed characters for storytelling

We hope you enjoy building and playing with your Lego Castle Set.
