VENDOR FAQ

This document provides answers to frequently asked questions for vendors selling on our platform.

1. PAYMENT PROCESS
Vendors receive payments within 5 business days after order delivery confirmation.
Payments are transferred directly to the registered bank account.

2. COMMISSION STRUCTURE
A standard commission fee is deducted from each sale. The percentage varies depending on product category.

3. SHIPPING RESPONSIBILITY
Vendors are responsible for packaging and shipping their products.
All shipments must be completed within the promised delivery timeline.

4. ORDER MANAGEMENT
Vendors can manage orders through the vendor dashboard:
- Accept or reject orders
- Update shipping status
- Track deliveries

5. RETURNS HANDLING
- Vendors must accept returns for eligible products.
- Returned items will be inspected before refund processing.
- Vendors may be responsible for return shipping costs in certain cases.

6. PRODUCT LISTING GUIDELINES
- Provide accurate descriptions and images.
- Avoid misleading or false claims.
- Ensure compliance with platform policies.

7. ACCOUNT SUSPENSION
Vendor accounts may be suspended for:
- Repeated order cancellations
- Poor customer ratings
- Violation of policies

8. CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION
Vendors are expected to:
- Respond to customer queries within 24 hours
- Maintain professional communication
- Resolve disputes promptly

9. SUPPORT
For vendor support, contact the vendor helpdesk via the dashboard.

We aim to create a reliable and transparent marketplace for all vendors.
